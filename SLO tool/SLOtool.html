Welcome to the IEA Student Learning Objective Interactive Template. The purpose of the SLO template is to help you, as an educator, organize the ideas you will need to meet the requirements of the student growth portion of your Illinois Performance Evaluation. This template is the result of a cooperative process involving active practitioners and Illinois Stakeholders who work in the area of student growth assessment. We hope you find it helpful.

The SLO Interactive template does not replace meeting with your evaluator to determine the elements you will use to measure student growth, but it does provide a step-by-step process for organizing those elements and saving them as a paper copy.

The template does not provide choices for assessments. Your district PERA joint committee will provide criteria and/or guidelines on which assessments you will use. If you are developing your own assessment, the district PERA joint committee should provide assessment criteria. . Please contact your district PERA joint committee for further guidance.

Key Features:

Drop-down menus. Throughout the template is an opportunity to offer responses for you to select. These are only suggestions. You may also, for any of the elements, type in a response if the drop-down list does not offer the response you need.

Help and Support. The template provides help on the elements for the student learning objective process. Simply roll the cursor over any of the highlighted text in the template and you’ll find definitions and links to websites offering help in understanding and using the element. The training hyperlinks provide more information on the elements and will aid you as you complete the SLO Interactive Template.

Saving Content. The template content has the option of being saved on your computer as a word document or print the document. We encourage the user to print the summary of the content upon completion. If you need to leave the tool hit the save button to load your answers into a text file. Then when you return upload the file and continue working. This feature will not work in Internet Explorer 9 and below.

We also recommend reviewing the summary document with your evaluator for input, feedback or approval. Once the evaluator approves the student growth measurement plan, both of you should sign and date the document. The plan includes a progress monitoring schedule. Please keep the document as a reminder of the timeline and a record of your student growth performance evaluation agreement.
